===== Seychelles Information =====

Seychelles, officially the Republic of Seychelles, is an archipelagic state consisting of 115 islands in the Indian Ocean. Its capital and largest city, Victoria, is 1,500 kilometres (800 nautical miles) east of mainland Africa. Nearby island countries and territories include the [[Comoros]], [[Madagascar]], [[Mauritius]], and the French overseas departments of [[Mayotte]] and [[Réunion]] to the south; and Maldives and the Chagos Archipelago (administered by the United Kingdom as the [[British Indian Ocean Territory]]) to the east.
