# Aerodrome Data

Hosea Kutako International Airport is the main international airport of Namibia, serving the capital city of Windhoek. The airport was previously named J.G. Strijdom Airport up until 1990. It has direct flights to Europe and most of Sub-Saharan Africa including Ethiopia. The airport is operated by Namibia Airports Company.
