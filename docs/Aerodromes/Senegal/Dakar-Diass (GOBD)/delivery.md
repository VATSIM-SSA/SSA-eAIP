# Delivery

!!! info
    Dakar-Diass does not have a dedicated Delivery frequency, as such all Delivery responsibilities are assumed by Diass Ground (GOBD_GND). Under no circumstances should a GOBD_DEL position ever be opened without prior approval from staff.

## Phraseology

SID
!!! info "Delivery Phraseology"
    "(callsign), cleared to (destination), (SID) departure, climb initially (FL), squawk (squawk)."

RWY Heading

!!! info "Delivery Phraseology"
    "(callsign), cleared to (destination), after departure runway (number), fly runway heading, climb initially (FL), squawk (squawk)."

## Other procedures

If the pilot is flying into the Dakar Oceanic FIR (GOOOO/GOOC), you may ask for a SELCAL code, if not put in their flight plan already. The aircrafts SELCAL code shall be inserted in the item 18 (RMK) of the Flight Plan, preceded by the “SEL” indicator.

!!! info "Example"
SEL/MSHR
