## Departure Procedures

!!! warning
    There are currently no operational SIDs at Cape Town. All aircraft are to expect Runway Track, climbing to FL90.

## Phraseology

**The passing altitude to be issued in clearances is 2000ft.**

SID
!!! info "Delivery Phraseology"
    "(callsign), cleared to (destination), FLxxx on request, after departure Runway XX, comply with the (SID), climb to FL70, passing 2000ft contact Cape Town Approach on 119.700, squawk (squawk)"

Runway Track
!!! info "Delivery Phraseology"
    "(callsign), cleared to (destination), FLxxx on request, after departure Runway XX, non standard, maintain runway track, climb to FL90, passing 2000ft contact Cape Town Approach on 119.700, squawk (squawk)"
