# Delivery

!!! info
    King Phalo does not have a dedicated Delivery frequency, as such all Delivery responsibilities are assumed by East London Tower (FAEL_TWR). Under no circumstances should a FAEL_DEL position ever be opened without prior approval from staff.

## Departure Procedures

Runway 11/29 , 06/24

!!! info "Standard Instrument Departures"
    There are no Standard Instrument Departures out of FAEL. All aircraft are to be given runway track with an initial climb of FL70.
    
## Phraseology

**The passing altitude to be issued in clearances is 2500ft.**

Runway Track
!!! info "Delivery Phraseology"
    "KQA841, cleared to (destination), FLXXX on request, after departure runway XX, non standard, maintain runway track, climb to FL70, after passing altiude 2500ft, contact East London Approach on 120.100, squawk XXXX."
