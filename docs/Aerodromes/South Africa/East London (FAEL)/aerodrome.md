# Aerodrome Data

King Phalo Airport (IATA: ELS, ICAO: FAEL) (Koning Phalo Lughawe) is an airport serving East London, a city in the Eastern Cape province on the southeast coast of South Africa. Formally known as East London Airport, it attracts 946,000 people to East London each year.
