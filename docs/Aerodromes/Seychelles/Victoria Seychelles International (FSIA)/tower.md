# Tower

The responsibility of Tower at Victoria Seychelles International falls to the dedicated Tower ATS unit, Seychelles Tower (FSIA_TWR) on 118.300. TWR will be responsible for the movements on the runway, as well as the responsibility of ensuring safety amongst VFR aircraft operating in the circuit or within the Victoria Seychelles International CTR from GND - 3500ft MSL.

## Visual Flight Rules (VFR) Aircraft

| Type | Circuit Altitude |
| :---------: | :---------: |
| Jet | 1000ft |
| Turbine / Piston | 1000ft |
| Helicopter | 1000ft |

**All circuits are conducted left hand normally, but may be conducted right hand at the discretion of the Tower controller.**  

!!! info "Circuit Clearance"
    ZSABC, hold position, after departure Runway XX, L / R hand circuits, not above altitude 1000ft, report L / R downwind Runway XX.

!!! info "Circuit Clearance (Non STD)"
    ZSABC, hold position, after departure Runway XX, non standard L / R hand circuits, not above altitude 1000ft, report non standard L / R downwind Runway XX.

### VRP Map

!!! info
    There are no published Visual Reference Points within the Seychelles CTR

### VFR Procedures

!!! info
    There are no standard VFR routings within the Seychelles CTR. 

!!! info "Entering the CTR"
    ZSABC, cleared to enter the Seychelles CTR from the N / S / E / W, not above altitude 1000ft, L / non standard R downwind runway XX."

!!! info "Exiting the CTR"
    ZSABC, hold position, after departure Runway XX, cleared to exit the Seychelles CTR to the N / S / E / W, not above altitude 1000ft, report leaving the CTR"

## Takeoff Phraseology

!!! info "Takeoff Clearance"
    KQA12W, Runway XX, wind 080 degrees at 9 knots, cleared for takeoff, report airborne.

!!! info "Takeoff (Intersection)"
    KQA12W, Runway XX via XX, wind 080 degrees at 9 knots, cleared for takeoff, report airborne.

!!! info "Handoff"
    KQA12W, contact Seychelles Approach 119.700.
