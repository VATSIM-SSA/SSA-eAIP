# Aerodrome Data
Moi International Airport (IATA: MBA, ICAO: HKMO) is an international airport in Mombasa, the second-largest city in Kenya. Moi International Airport serves the city of Mombasa and surrounding communities. It was named after former Kenyan President Daniel arap Moi during his tenure.
