# Aerodrome Data

The Lomé–Tokoin Gnassingbé Eyadéma International Airport (IATA: LFW, ICAO: DXXX) is an international airport serving Lomé, the capital of Togo. The airport is named after revolutionary and second president of Togo, Gnassingbé Eyadéma, who served from 1967 to 2005. The airport is a main operating base for ASKY Airlines.
