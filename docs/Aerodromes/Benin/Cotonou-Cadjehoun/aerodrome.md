# Aerodrome Data

The Cotonou Cardinal Bernadin Gantin International Airport (IATA: COO, ICAO: DBBB) is an international airport serving Cotonou and Porto-Novo, the capital of Bénin. The airport is situated in the Cotonou neighbourhood of Cadjehoun and is named after Cardinal Bernadin Gantin who served as a Cardinal to the Catholic Church.
