# Robert Gabriel Mugabe International Airport (FVRG)

Robert Gabriel Mugabe International Airport, colloquially known as "RGM" or Mugabe Airport, formerly known as Salisbury Airport, is an international airport in Harare, Zimbabwe. It is the largest airport in the country and serves as the base for Air Zimbabwe. The airport is operated by the Civil Aviation Authority of Zimbabwe.
