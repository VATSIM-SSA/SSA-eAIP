\# Delivery

!!! info Abuja does not have a dedicated Delivery frequency, as such all
Delivery responsibilities are assumed by Abuja Ground (DNAA_GND). Under
no circumstances should a DNAA_DEL position ever be opened without prior
approval from staff.

\## Departure Procedures

Pilots shall be issued one of the RNAV departures when flying out of
Abuja. This process is done automatically through the vSID plugin on
EuroScope.

In the event a pilot is unable to comply, a non standard runway track
departure or other means should be issued to the pilot.

\*\*Runway 04\*\*

\| Fix \| Procedure \| \| :\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--: \| :\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--: \|
\| IKSEM \| IKSEM1S \| \| IKVAD \| IKVAD1S \| \| OKORU \| OKORU1S \|

\*\*Runway 22\*\*

\| Fix \| Procedure \| \| :\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--: \| :\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--: \|
\| IKSEM \| IKSEM1Q \| \| IKVAD \| IKVAD1Q \| \| OKORU \| OKORU1Q \|

\## Initial Climb

The Initial Climb provided within the clearance will be set at FL110.
