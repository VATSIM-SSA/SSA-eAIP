# FASA General Procedures

This page provides an overview of published Standard Instrument Departures (SIDs), Standard Terminal Arrival Routes (STARs), and approach procedures applicable to common airports within the FASA region. Procedures that are currently suspended are indicated by a strike-through and are not in operational use.

The information presented reflects procedures in common operational use and is provided to assist pilots and air traffic service personnel. Operations shall be conducted in accordance with the latest applicable aeronautical information and ATC clearances. Information below is updated monthly or as required to reflect changes in the NOTAMS.
