# 1. Johannesburg Oceanic

 The Johannesburg Oceanic FIR covers a significant oceanic segment of the South Atlantic. It is neighbored by SAVF Comodoro Rivadavia FIR, SAEF Ezeiza FIR, SUEO Montevideo FIR, SBAO Atlantico FIR to the West,  Luanda FIR,  Windhoek FIR,  Cape Town FIR,  Johannesburg FIR,  Beira FIR,  Antananarivo FIR and  Mauritius FIR to the North and YMMM Melbourne Oceanic to the East.

 It should be noted that the Atlantic component of the FAJO Johannesburg Oceanic FIR is part of the Atlantic Ocean Random Routing RNAV Area.
